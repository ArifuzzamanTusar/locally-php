Lets talk about our Key features

In present people wants to know what is happening, where is happening, when happening and many questions. And also, they want to know about latest update of any past incidence. So, we can update news, posts & topics on our website and the people can knows the matter about any incidence on our website. We can give them update news on any subject like Local guides, Help-seeking, Discussion and many others.

Area-based Feeds

Locally organizes users' feeds according to their area. So they can easily explore their city’s food, culture,local-guides, news, reviews, discussions, advertisements and so many.

Well organized experience

Locally allows users to explore according to their interests. It has many topics to explore, discuss & learn. The notification feature allows comment/appreciations notice to make the experience better and bigger.

Well communicative

Locally allows users to make a discussion according to a topic, also can comment and appreciate other users’ posts, they are able to see others profile data, their previous posts, and so on.

Secured & well maintained

Locally is strictly responsible for allowing user antispam & clean user experience. It has an admin panel to verify users according to their NID also maintain users & their behavior. It has well-managed report system. Any suspicious behavior might result in a permanently ban.
